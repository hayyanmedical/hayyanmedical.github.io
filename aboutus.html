About Hayyan

Connecting patients from around the world to exceptional medical care and the vibrant culture of Iran.

Our Story

At Hayyan, we are passionate about making world-class healthcare accessible. Founded to bridge the gap between international patients and Iran's top medical expertise, we offer personalized treatment plans combined with unforgettable cultural experiences.

Our Purpose

Mission

To deliver affordable, high-quality medical care with a seamless travel experience, showcasing Iran's renowned hospitality.

Vision

We envision a future where every individual enjoys the gift of health, everywhere.🌍💚

Our Journey

"As a pioneering medical tourism startup established in 2025, Hayyan is committed to redefining exceptional healthcare experiences in Iran."

While we are newly launched, our vision is bold: to become a global bridge connecting international patients to Iran's world-class medical expertise, cutting-edge treatments, and warm hospitality.

Guided by Iran's leading hospitals and specialists, we're building a future where every patient receives affordable, high-quality care—paired with the chance to discover Iran's rich culture.

This is just the beginning of our journey, and we invite you to grow with us.
